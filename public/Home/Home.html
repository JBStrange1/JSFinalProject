Wheely Good BBQ began as a passion project for Erich, a young chef with a love for barbecue and a dream of sharing it with his community. Facing financial constraints, Erich transformed an old delivery van into a mobile kitchen, where he served up mouthwatering smoked meats and savory sides. Through hard work and dedication, Erich's food truck quickly gained popularity, becoming a beloved fixture in the town and beyond.
